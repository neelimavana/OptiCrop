About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is a machine-learning powered crop recommendation system that helps farmers and researchers make data-driven farming decisions.

It's trained on soil nutrient levels (N, P, K), climate conditions (temperature, humidity, rainfall), and soil pH. Logistic Regression, KNN, Decision Tree, and Random Forest were trained and compared — Random Forest performed best at 99.5% accuracy and was selected for deployment.
